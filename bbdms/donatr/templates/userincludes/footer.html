About

The Blood Bank Donor Management System is a web-based platform built with Python Django and MySQL to efficiently manage blood donations, donors, and requests. It connects donors and recipients through a secure and easy-to-use interface, allowing admins to track inventory, handle emergency needs, and manage donor data. Donors can register, view history, and get alerts, while recipients can request blood directly through the system.
